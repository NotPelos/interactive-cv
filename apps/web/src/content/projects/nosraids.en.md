Running raids in an MMO with hundreds of players is pure entropy without the right tool. NosRaids fixes that: guild leaders organize groups, assign roles and coordinate timing — no micromanaging over Discord at midnight.

Auth with role-based access — leaders configure, everyone else reads. Bilingual ES/EN with the same i18n pattern I use in this CV: not auto-translated, content adapted per language.

Deployed on Vercel because sometimes the boring choice is the right one.
